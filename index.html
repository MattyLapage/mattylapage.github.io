Hi! I do Inclusive Design

Matt Lepage
UX Design & Accessibility

Product Design, with Access in Mind

I’m a Brooklyn-based Product Designer, working with design and product teams to build inclusive experiences that work better for everyone. I’m excited by novel modes of interaction, and the ways that interface design can reshape our relation to technology and the world around us.

Currently: Senior A11Y Designer at 2U Inc

Right now I'm working with designers and devs to chart a course for accessible learning experiences online. My work centers around incorporating accessibility and inclusive design practices into our UX org.

Previously: Senior UX Design, Front-End Development

Prior to my current role, I worked at 2U as a Senior Product Designer, specializing in accessibility topics and CMS design. My largest projects were modular end user experiences and tools for building them, like a forms UI rebuild and accompanying CMS.

On The Side: Indie Gaming Events and Livestreams

Outside of work hours I help run an annual Indie Arcade festival, building out livestream assets and talking to fun people about weird games.
