Accessibility Design Consulting

Senior Accessibility Designer, 2U Inc, 2020 - Present

Summary

Currently acting as internal consultant to shift accessibility conversations to the left in our product lifecycle. This includes meeting with product leaders for scoping, acting as a member of the product design team to bake inclusive practices into UX workflows, and running cross organization workshops to build accessibility knowledge.

Project Goals

Advocate accessibility across org structure, educating design teams for the most impact

Responsibilities

Product management, Accessibility Testing & Engineering, Code Prototyping, User Testing, Advocacy, Documentation

Detailed Process

Designed curriculum, presentations and workshops to promote inclusive thinking across product teams. Built and ran training for development and UX teams to build a shared understanding of how our designs and code can best meet the diverse needs of our users, shifting the accessibility conversation away from post-production compliance audits and towards holistic planning.

Contributed designs and usage documentation for a growing design system, particularly around interactive components. Paired with UX team to build stable atomic foundations for design system color and typography, and worked with engineering to codify interactions for complex components.

Coordinated with compliance team to facilitate VPATs, leading user mapping sessions with product leadership to build out a complete understanding of the user flows students rely on to participate fully in online degrees, and using auditor feedback to write development tickets and pair with developers to address accessibility debt across those flows.
